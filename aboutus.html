Our History

We're a mother-daughter duo who found ourselves with plenty of time on our hands due to the imposition of the nation-wide lockdown courtesy of Covid-19. With hands excited to whip up new dishes and the internet on our side, we were ready to blast off. Over the course of 4 months, my mother rediscovered her passion for baking.
Scouting for new recipes everyday, Rupal Lavana is the head of operations and executive chef while I, Rrutum Lavana, am her sous chef. With occassional help from my sister Samprati Lavana, we have managed to cook 60+ new dishes in the span of 4 months!

This webpage showcases all the dishes we've had the pleasure of cooking together in quarantine. Hence the name Quarantine Kitchen!

Meet the QK Family

Rupal Lavana Head of Operations & Executive Chef

Our Head of Operations, Rupal, is responsible for sourcing the raw materials and ingredients along with scouting for lip-smacking recipes. Working tirelessly in the kitchen from a very young age, one could say that Rupal spent her entire life gearing up for this lockdown. Having ample of time on her hands for the first time in her life, she didn't decide to just kick back and relax. Instead, she embarked on a mission to indulge herself and her family with food they had never prepared before. She revisited her passion for baking after almost 25 years. Our executive chef has a knack for cooking and adds a personal touch to every dish she prepares. Amma Mia!
