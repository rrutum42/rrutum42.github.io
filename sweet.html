Naan Khatai

1 cup ghee
1 cup wheat flour
3/4 cup rava (fine)
3/4 cup maida
2 TSP gram flour
1 cup powdered sugar
2 pinch baking powder
1 pinch baking soda


Steps:
1. Add ghee and sieved powdered sigar and whisk till it becomes light and fluffy
2. Add dry ingredients after sieving.
3. Mix till you can make and shape small sections. Can add some milk if the mixture is too dry.
4. Grease the tray and put in oven at 180*C for 20 minutes then flip and again for 20 minutes, again flip and keep for 5 mins

Shakarpara

1 cup ghee
1 cup sugar
1 cup water
1. Mix these 3 ingredients well,once sugar dissolves add whole wheat flour to the mixture till it forms in a dough.
2.Roll small portion cut and deep fry or bake.
Note:i. Oil should be hot at first every time then deep fry at low flame.
ii. At first it might seems chewy but it will turn crispy after a while.

Hot Chocolate-Coffee

1 cup Almonds
Crush but not totally
1/2 cup Wheat Flour
1 cup Jaggery
Heat 1 cup Ghee
After a little heated add and mix
Add Wheat Flour
Mix till reddish/brown
Heat 1 cup water on side
Add water very slowly and keep stirring
Finally add Jaggery and mix well till ghee comes out
Turn off stove
